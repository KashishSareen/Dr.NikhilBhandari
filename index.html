About the Doctor

Dedicated to Every Child's Well-Being

Dr. Nikhil Bhandari is a Senior Consultant Pediatrician & Neonatologist with 15+ years of clinical experience. He has been serving children and families in Andheri East for over 14 years — building lasting trust with thousands of parents.

With advanced qualifications and a deeply compassionate approach, Dr. Bhandari ensures that every child receives personalised, evidence-based care in a warm and reassuring environment.

Special Interests

Areas of Special Focus

Targeted expertise in conditions that matter most to your child's long-term health and quality of life.

Allergy & Asthma Care

Child Nutrition

What We Offer

Our Medical Services

Comprehensive pediatric services designed to support your child from birth through adolescence.

Vaccination
